Checks for a given matrix whether the binary k-separation is also a ternary one.

Checks, for a ternary input matrix \( M \) and a k-separation ( \( k \in \{1,2,3\} \)) of the (binary) support matrix of \( M \), whether it is also a k-separation of \( M \) itself. The result is stored in *pisTernary.

If the check fails, a certifying submatrix is returned.

Parameters
cmrCMR environment.
sepaSeparation.
matrixMatrix.
submatrixSubmatrix of matrix to consider (may be NULL).
pisTernaryPointer for storing whether the check passed.
psubmatrixPointer for storing a violator submatrix (may be NULL).

Constructs the 2-sum of the two matrices first and second via firstMarker and secondMarker.

Let \( A \) and \( B \) denote the matrices given by first and second. If firstMarker indexes a row vector \( a^\intercal \) of \( A \) then secondMarker must index a column vector \( b \) of \( B \). Then the 2-sum is the matrix

\[ \begin{pmatrix} A & \mathbb{O} \\ b a^\intercal & B \end{pmatrix}. \]

Otherwise, firstMarker must index a column vector \( a \) of \( A \) and secondMarker must index a row vector \( b^\intercal \) of \( B \), and the 2-sum is the matrix

\[ \begin{pmatrix} A & a b^\intercal \\ \mathbb{O} & B \end{pmatrix}. \]

The resulting matrix is created and stored in *presult.

cmrCMR environment.
firstFirst matrix.
secondSecond matrix.
firstMarkerMarker element of first matrix.
secondMarkerMarker element of second matrix.
